What Is Eating Healthy?

Professionals today says eating healthy means eating a variety of food that gives you the nutrients you need to maintain your health, feel good, have energy, and prevent diseases. Protein, fat, water, carbohydrates, vitamins, and minerals are all the good nutrients youl'll need to eat healthy. Some of the major food groups are : vegetables, and legumes or beans, fruit, lean meats and poultry, fish, eggs, tofu, nuts, and seeds, grain foods, mostlt whole grain or high cereal fiber varieties, milk, yogurt, cheese or alternatives, mostly reduce fat.
